WHAT TO BRING

HEALTH GUIDELINES

Please bring a labeled bag that includes a pacifier (if your child uses one), at least two diapers, a change of clothes, and all personal items your child may need such as wipes, ointment, bottles, and/or comforting blankets. All personal items should be labeled before arrival. If you forget diapers and wipes, we have some on hand.

Water, saltines, and graham crackers are available.
