HEALTH GUIDELINES

In bringing your child into our care, we ask that you follow a few required safety procedures:

  1. Sign in on the sheet outside the Nursery entrance each Sunday.
  2. Please be sure to inform the nursery staff if your child has any allergies or dietary or other special needs.
  3. Fill out a nametag for your child and place it on his or her back (they're less likely to remove it from this location).
  4. Enter only through the door marked â€œEntrance."
  5. You are welcome to stay until your child is comfortable, but keep in mind that most children settle in a few minutes after your departure.
  6. We require that whichever parent signs in their child or children be the same parent who retrieves them.
